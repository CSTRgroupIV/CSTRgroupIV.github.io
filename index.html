About Poverty

Poverty is a complex social issue that affects millions around the world. It is characterized by a lack of resources, insufficient access to basic needs such as food, shelter, and education, and limited opportunities for economic advancement.

Statistics

Here are some current statistics about poverty:

  • Over 700 million people live on less than $1.90 a day.
  • About 10% of the world's population is considered to be in extreme poverty.
  • Children are disproportionately affected, with 1 in 6 children living in extreme poverty.
